## Is a 2000–3000 Watt Generator Right for You?

A 2000–3000 watt portable generator is ideal for powering essential household appliances during short power outages or light backup scenarios.

### Common Appliances You Can Run

- Refrigerator / Freezer
- Microwave Oven
- LED Lights
- Wi-Fi Router
- Laptop or TV

### Appliances It Cannot Reliably Run

- Central Air Conditioner
- Electric Clothes Dryer
- Electric Stove
- Large Space Heaters

### Typical Use Cases

- Home backup during short outages
- Apartment or condo emergency power
- RV and camping (with inverter generators)

### Inverter or Conventional Generator?

For most users in this wattage range, inverter generators are preferred because they are quieter, more fuel-efficient, and safer for electronics.

This recommendation is based on total running watts, peak startup surge, and a built-in safety margin.
