## Inverter vs Conventional Generators in This Range

Both inverter and conventional models are widely available at this size.

**Inverter generators**

- Quieter operation
- Better fuel efficiency at partial load
- Safer for sensitive electronics
- Often more compact

**Conventional generators**

- Lower upfront cost
- Higher raw output per dollar
- Louder operation

For residential or RV use, inverter models are often preferred, while conventional generators may suit budget-focused buyers.
