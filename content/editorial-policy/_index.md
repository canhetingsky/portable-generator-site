## Editorial Policy

Portable Generator Guide is committed to providing accurate, safety-focused, and independent information about portable generators.

This editorial policy outlines how content is created, reviewed, and maintained.

---

## Content Creation Standards

All articles are written with the following priorities:

- Clear explanations suitable for non-experts  
- Emphasis on real-world use and safety risks  
- Avoidance of exaggerated performance claims  
- Neutral language free from marketing pressure  

Technical topics are explained using commonly accepted industry terminology while remaining accessible to general readers.

---

## Sources and References

Content development may include information derived from:

- Manufacturer manuals and technical documentation  
- Public safety guidelines  
- Industry-standard electrical practices  
- Widely accepted engineering principles  

Articles are written to reflect general best practices rather than brand-specific instructions.

---

## Content Review and Updates

Generator technology and safety recommendations can change over time.

To maintain accuracy:

- Content is reviewed periodically  
- Articles may be updated to reflect new safety guidance  
- Outdated or misleading information is revised or removed  

Publication dates do not imply permanent accuracy.

---

## Independence and Transparency

Portable Generator Guide operates independently.

- No manufacturers control editorial decisions  
- Product mentions are informational, not promotional  
- Recommendations prioritize safety and suitability  

Any future monetization will not influence editorial standards.

---

## Reader Responsibility

Information on this site is intended for general educational purposes.

Readers are responsible for following manufacturer instructions, local regulations, and professional advice when operating electrical equipment.
