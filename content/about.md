## About Portable Generator Guide

Portable Generator Guide is an independent educational website focused on explaining how portable generators work, how they are used, and how to operate them safely.

The goal of this site is to provide clear, practical information for people who want to understand portable generators without marketing pressure or unnecessary technical complexity.

---

## What This Site Covers

This site focuses on educational topics related to portable generators, including:

- How portable generators work
- Common use cases such as home backup power, camping, and emergency situations
- Power requirements and limitations
- Generator types, fuel options, and basic comparisons
- Safety risks and proper operating practices

This site does **not** provide product rankings, pricing advice, or brand endorsements.

---

## Who This Site Is For

Portable Generator Guide is intended for:

- Homeowners preparing for power outages
- First-time portable generator users
- Campers, RV users, and outdoor enthusiasts
- Anyone seeking clear, unbiased explanations of generator basics

---

## Independence & Monetization

Portable Generator Guide does not sell generators directly.

Some pages may reference third-party products or services for informational purposes, and in the future the site may include affiliate links.  
Any monetization will not influence the accuracy, independence, or educational intent of the content.

---

## Safety Disclaimer

Portable generators involve fuel, electricity, and exhaust gases. Improper use can result in serious injury or death.

All content on this site is provided for general educational purposes only and should not be considered professional or technical advice.  
Always follow manufacturer instructions and consult qualified professionals when appropriate.
